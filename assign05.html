F1 Grid

2021 F1 Grid

This is the grid for the 2021 season of F1

Mercedes

Mercedes have been really strong during the whole turbo v6 era of F1, this has seen them win 7 constructor championships with 6 being back to back. Their star driver Lewis Hamilton has 7 driver championships to his name, currently he sits in 2nd to Redbull's Max Verstappen in the driver fight. Right now the team sits in 1st with 433.5 points. Their engine supplier is Mercedes.

Redbull

Redbull has had a strong season so far. Their young driver, Max Verstappen, is in a tight title fight with Mercedes driver Lewis Hamilton, and are seen to be trading off the lead after each race. Right now they sit in 2nd with 397.5 points. Their engine supplier is Honda.

Ferrari

Ferrari hasn't been as strong as they once were. They have an all young driver lineup with Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz JR. They are currently 4th in the standings with 232.5 points. Their engine supplier is Ferrari.

McLaren

McLaren has had a rough couple of seasons but have been carried by their young driver Lando Norris. They were a backmarker with the likes of Haas a couple years back. Right now they stand 3rd in the standings with 240 points. Their engine supplier is Mercedes.

Alpha Tauri

Alpha Tauri used to be known as Torro Rosso before they rebranded. They're a sister team to Redbull and the drivers for them feed into Redbull. They currently sit in 6th in the standings with 92 points. Their engine supplier is Honda.

Aston Martin

Aston Martin was Force India before they went bankrupt. Aston Martin picked up ex Ferrari driver Sebastian Vettel, who has 4 world championships. They sit at 7th in the standings with 61 points. Their engine supplier is Mercedes.

Alpine

Alpine was Renault last year and technically still is but now is rebranded to their sport name. Alpine picked up Fernando Alonso out of retirement, he has 2 world championships to his name. Right now they sit in 5th in the standings with a points haul of 104 points. Their engine supplier is Renault.

Alfa Romero

Alfa Romero, who is owned by Sauber has Kimi Raikkonen, an experienced driver with one world championship to his name. Next season Valtteri Bottas is contracted to replace Raikkonen in due to his retirement. Right now the team has 7 points which sits them at 9th with 7 points. Their engine supplier is Ferrari.

Williams

Williams was a back running team because of their lack of money for development. They were bought by Dorrilton recently from the Williams family. Recently they have had a constant run of points with George Russell, who's contracted to drive for Mercedes next season. They currently sit at 8th in the standings with a haul of 23 points. Their engine supplier is Mercedes.

Haas

Haas is an American team with two rookies this season. Cuurently they sit at the bottom of the standings with no points achieved. They are back runners because they did no development changes to the car but instead put all their efforts to the 2022 car development. Their engine supplier is Ferrari.

Grid subject to change for the 2022 season. All opinions are of my own.
