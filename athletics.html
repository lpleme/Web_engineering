BBYC University's Athletics and Their History

History

We have the worlds leading athletic program. We are in the D1 conference for almost every sport. Our coaches are top of the line and come from professional teams. 90% of our athletes graduate into a professional team.

Sports

We offer a multitude of sports that most other universities don't offer. We have a horseback riding team, a university driven race team in the Formula SAC series, and we also have an eSports team that competes in many different games. We also have the normal sports like football, soccer, lacrosse, wrestling, tennis, basketball, and baseball.

Veni Vidi Vici

We came, we saw, we conquered. This is our athletic departments motto. We conquer everything we set our eyes on. We have won almost every division since entering into there respective programs.
